Customized Learning

Every student’s math journey is unique. I create a personalized plan based on each student’s goals, learning style, and pace. Together, we will:

  • Reinforce core concepts through hands-on practice
  • Build confidence with step-by-step problem-solving
  • Develop test-taking strategies and time management
  • Apply math to real-world scenarios to keep learning fun

What a Tutoring Session Looks Like

My sessions are always one-on-one and in-person. In each session, we:

  • Identify the specific concepts to focus on (e.g., homework, upcoming tests)
  • Review any foundational skills needed to understand the new material
  • Work through example problems and practice together
  • Discuss tips, shortcuts, and strategies for mastering the topic

Sessions typically last 60-90 minutes, but I can adjust the duration based on individual needs.
